To keep WorldIDP safe and trustworthy for everyone, this policy sets clear rules on how our Services may and may not be used.

1Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy sets out the rules for using the WorldIDP website and services. It exists to keep our platform safe, lawful, and trustworthy for everyone. By using our Services, you agree to follow these rules.

2Prohibited activities

You must not:

  • Submit false, altered, forged, or stolen documents, or documents that are not your own.
  • Provide inaccurate, misleading, or fraudulent information.
  • Use the Services for any unlawful, fraudulent, or deceptive purpose.
  • Impersonate another person or misrepresent your identity.
  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems, accounts, or data.
  • Introduce malware, viruses, or any harmful code.
  • Interfere with, disrupt, or overload our website or infrastructure.
  • Scrape, copy, or harvest data from our site without permission.
  • Resell or commercially exploit our Services without authorization.

3Your responsibilities

You are responsible for the accuracy and legality of everything you submit, and for keeping your account and order information secure. You must comply with all applicable laws when using our Services.

4Consequences of violations

Violations have consequences. We may suspend or terminate access, cancel orders, refuse service, and report unlawful activity to the relevant authorities where we detect a breach of this policy.

5Reporting misuse

If you become aware of any misuse of our Services, please report it to hello@worldidp.com so we can investigate.
