      Order journey

      How your printed permit reaches you.

      Every printed package moves through the same five stages. Here's what each one means.

      1. 1

        Order confirmed

        We received your order and your payment was confirmed.

      2. 2

        In processing

        Your printed booklet and ID card are being prepared.

      3. 3

        Shipped

        Your package gets a tracking number and is handed to the carrier.

      4. 4

        In transit

        Your package moves through the carrier network toward you.

      5. 5

        Delivered

        Your printed IDP package arrives at your address.
