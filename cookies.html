Cookie Policy

How worldidp.com may use browser storage and similar technologies.

Last updated: July 11, 2026
On this pageWhat cookies areTypes usedYour choicesContact

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small files or browser storage entries that help a website remember information about a visit. Similar technologies include local storage and session storage.

2. Types we may use

Strictly necessary

These support essential functions such as navigation, security, load balancing and form operation. The site may not work correctly without them.

Basic performance

If enabled, these help us understand aggregated visits, errors and page performance. They should not be used to collect unnecessary sensitive information.

3. No document-file cookies

This website does not use cookies to store document files.

4. Your choices

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Some strictly necessary features may not work correctly if all storage is disabled.

5. Updates

The website owner should keep this policy aligned with the tools actually deployed on the live website.
