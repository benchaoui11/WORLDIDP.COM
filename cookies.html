Cookie Policy

How worldidp.com may use browser storage and similar technologies.

Last updated: July 6, 2026
On this pageWhat cookies areTypes usedYour choicesContact

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored by a browser. Similar technologies include local storage, pixels and tags. They can support security, remember choices and help measure website use.

2. Types we may use

Strictly necessary

These support essential functions such as navigation, security, fraud prevention, load balancing, consent preferences and form operation. The site may not work correctly without them.

Analytics and performance

With consent where required, these help us understand aggregated visits, errors and page performance. Providers may process device and usage information under their own terms.

Advertising measurement

If enabled and legally permitted, advertising platforms may measure whether an advertisement led to a website visit or action. We do not need these cookies to provide the basic site. They should be activated only after required consent choices are implemented.

3. Cookie duration

Session cookies expire when the browser closes. Persistent cookies remain until their stated expiry or deletion. Duration depends on purpose and provider.

4. Your choices

You can use an available consent control to accept or reject non-essential categories. Browser settings can also block or delete cookies. Blocking essential storage may affect operation. Where applicable, advertising providers offer their own opt-out controls.

5. Implementation note

The website owner must keep the live cookie banner and cookie inventory aligned with the analytics, advertising and payment tools actually deployed. This policy must be updated when those tools change.
