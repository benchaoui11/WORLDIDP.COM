# WorldIDP Translation Landing Page

Static, deployment-ready website for `worldidp.com`.

## Publish

Upload the contents of this folder to the web root. `index.html` is the landing page.

## Start button

`Start now` opens `/apply`. The form prepares an email addressed to `hello@worldidp.com`; it does not upload documents or store form data. Replace this workflow only after a secure intake system and its privacy disclosures are ready.

## Before advertising

- Confirm the legal company name, postal address, phone number and email are current.
- Confirm the service description exactly matches the service actually delivered.
- Add prices only when they are final and include all mandatory fees.
- If analytics, advertising pixels or non-essential cookies are installed, deploy a consent system where required and update `cookies.html` and `privacy.html`.
- Ensure HTTPS works on every page and test the complete order and support process.
- Do not advertise this service as a government-issued International Driving Permit, official licence, certified translation or guaranteed-to-be-accepted document unless that statement is independently true and documented.
- Have qualified legal counsel review the legal pages for the countries where the service is sold.

No website can guarantee Google Ads approval or prevent future suspension. Approval also depends on the advertisement, account history, billing, business verification, actual service and post-click experience.
