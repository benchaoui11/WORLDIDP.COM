What is an International Driving Permit?

An IDP is generally an official companion document linked to a valid national driving document. Issuing rules and requirements depend on the relevant country and authority.

The essential distinction

Official document or private translation?

An official permit can only be issued through a body authorized under the applicable rules. A private translation is simply a language aid prepared from readable source information.

WorldIDP is an independent translation company. We do not issue official documents or driving privileges, and our work never replaces the valid original.

Before relying on any document

Check the requirement at the source

Ask the relevant government authority and the organization receiving the document. Do not rely on an advertisement, private website or rental-desk assumption alone.

01 / DESTINATION

Check the country or region

Rules can differ by country, state, province or territory and can change over time.

02 / RECIPIENT

Ask the intended recipient

Confirm whether it requires an IDP, certified translation, private translation or another document.

03 / SOURCE

Keep the original valid

No translation creates validity, adds rights or fixes an expired or restricted source document.
