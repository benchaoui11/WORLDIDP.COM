At WorldIDP, protecting your personal information is a responsibility we take seriously. This policy explains in clear language what we collect, why we collect it, who we share it with, and the control you have over your data.

1Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy explains how WORLDIDP INTERNATIONAL LLC ("WorldIDP", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information when you visit worldidp.com, place an order, or otherwise interact with our services (together, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

2Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information so we can prepare and deliver your International Driving Document and operate our business:

a. Information you provide directly

  • Identity & contact details — first and last name, email address, phone number, and (for printed orders) your full postal address.
  • Order details — destination country, document validity term, license category, and product format (digital or print + digital).
  • Documents you upload — a passport-style photo, images of the front and back of your national driver's license, and your signature, which are required to prepare your translation document.

b. Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data — IP address, browser type, device type, pages viewed, and approximate location derived from your IP.
  • Cookies & similar technologies — see our Cookie Policy for full details.

c. Payment information

We do not collect payment at the time you submit your application. Our team reviews your documents first, then sends secure payment instructions by email. We never see or store your full card number — any payment you make afterward is handled directly by a PCI-DSS-compliant payment processor.

3How we use your information

We use your information only for legitimate business purposes, including to:

  • Prepare, produce, and deliver your International Driving Document.
  • Process payments and prevent fraudulent transactions.
  • Send order confirmations, delivery updates, and customer-support responses.
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide 24/7 support.
  • Improve and secure our website and Services.
  • Comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations.

We do not sell your data. WorldIDP does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing.

4How we share information

We share personal information only with trusted parties who help us run our Services, and only to the extent necessary:

Payment processorsOnce your application is approved, to securely charge your selected payment method. They handle card data under their own privacy terms.
Hosting & storageCloud infrastructure and database providers that host our website and securely store your order data and uploaded documents.
Shipping partnersFor printed orders, your name and address are shared with carriers to deliver your physical document.
Legal authoritiesWhere required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

We require every processor to safeguard your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5Data retention

We keep your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, including legal, tax, and accounting requirements. Uploaded identity documents are retained only for the period needed to produce and support your order, after which they are deleted or anonymized in line with our retention schedule.

6Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the EU/UK GDPR or the California CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Request a portable copy of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@worldidp.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

7Cookies, analytics & advertising

We and selected partners (including Google) use cookies and similar technologies to run the site, measure traffic, and show relevant advertising. Some partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other sites.

  • You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings and our cookie banner.
  • You can opt out of personalized Google advertising via Google Ad Settings.
  • You can opt out of many third-party advertising cookies via aboutads.info (US) or Your Online Choices (EU).

For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

8How we protect your data

We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections, access controls, and secure cloud storage. While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we work continuously to safeguard your data.

9Children's privacy

Our Services are intended for adults who hold a valid driver's license. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10International data transfers

WorldIDP operates internationally. Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. Where required, we put appropriate safeguards in place for such transfers in line with applicable data-protection laws.

11Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision. Significant changes will be highlighted on this page. Your continued use of the Services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
