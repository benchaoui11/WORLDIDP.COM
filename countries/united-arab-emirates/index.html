Do You Need an International Driving Permit in the United Arab Emirates?

Visitors may drive with a valid international driving licence or a foreign licence from an exempted/recognised country under UAE rules. The United Arab Emirates is party to the 1949 Geneva Convention and 1968 Vienna Convention. Traffic drives on the right. Sources reviewed 2026-07-23.

Who needs an IDP in the United Arab Emirates

Who this applies to. Visitors may drive with a valid international driving licence or a foreign licence from an exempted/recognised country under UAE rules.

Who is treated differently. Some foreign licences are recognised or exempted by UAE authorities.

The UAE distinguishes between visitors from countries whose licences are recognised or exempted and everyone else. Residents are treated differently from visitors, and the rules for converting to a UAE licence are separate again.

Which convention applies

The United Arab Emirates is party to the 1949 Geneva Convention and 1968 Vienna Convention. The convention determines which IDP format has a legal basis here, which is why two IDPs that look similar are not always interchangeable.

Where a country is party to more than one convention, the more recent convention generally governs between states that are party to both.

Is a digital IDP accepted in the United Arab Emirates?

Not confirmed. No confirmed official national policy was found. Carry the printed document and original licence when a physical document is requested.

This is the least documented question in this category. Most national authorities have not published a position on digital-only driving documents, and rental companies set their own rules. Carry a printed document and treat any digital copy as a convenience, not a substitute. See our explanation of what an IDP is for more.

What to carry

  • Original valid driving licence
  • Passport or travel ID where requested
  • IDP, official translation, or private translation-support document where applicable

Your original national driving licence is the document that grants driving privileges. A translation-support document is presented alongside it and never replaces it.

Rental desks and roadside checks in the United Arab Emirates

At the rental desk. UAE rental providers check documentation closely and the exempted-country list is applied at the desk. Confirm your licence origin against the current list before travelling.

In a roadside check. Traffic enforcement in the UAE is heavily automated and penalties are significant. Requirements are set out in the Federal Traffic Law and its Executive Regulation.

Specific to the United Arab Emirates

  • The UAE is party to both the 1949 Geneva and 1968 Vienna Conventions.
  • Some foreign licences are recognised or exempted under UAE rules; others require an international driving licence.
  • Rules for residents differ from rules for visitors.

Do I need an International Driving Permit in the UAE?

Are the rules different for UAE residents?

Yes. Residents are subject to separate licensing and conversion rules rather than the visitor framework.
