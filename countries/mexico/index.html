Do You Need an International Driving Permit in Mexico?

Visitors should confirm requirements with the destination authority, embassy, consulate, and rental provider before driving. Mexico is party to the 1926 Paris Convention. Traffic drives on the right. Motorway limit 110 km/h. Sources reviewed 2026-07-23.

Who needs an IDP in Mexico

Who this applies to. Visitors should confirm requirements with the destination authority, embassy, consulate, and rental provider before driving.

Who is treated differently. Some visitor licences, such as UK photocard licences under UK guidance, may be usable without an IDP.

Requirements in Mexico depend on your licence origin and on the state you drive in. UK guidance, for example, indicates a UK photocard licence may be usable without an IDP. Other licence origins should confirm directly.

Which convention applies

Mexico is party to the 1926 Paris Convention. The convention determines which IDP format has a legal basis here, which is why two IDPs that look similar are not always interchangeable.

Is a digital IDP accepted in Mexico?

Not confirmed. No confirmed official national policy was found. Carry the printed document and original licence when a physical document is requested.

This is the least documented question in this category. Most national authorities have not published a position on digital-only driving documents, and rental companies set their own rules. Carry a printed document and treat any digital copy as a convenience, not a substitute. See our explanation of what an IDP is for more.

What to carry

  • Original valid driving licence
  • Passport or travel ID where requested
  • IDP, official translation, or private translation-support document where applicable

Your original national driving licence is the document that grants driving privileges. A translation-support document is presented alongside it and never replaces it.

Rental desks and roadside checks in Mexico

At the rental desk. Mexican rental providers set their own documentation rules and mandatory local insurance requirements, which are often the more significant constraint for visitors.

In a roadside check. Enforcement varies significantly between federal highways and state or municipal roads. Carry the original licence at all times.

Specific to Mexico

  • Mexico's treaty position sits under the 1926 Paris Convention framework rather than the 1949 or 1968 conventions.
  • Mandatory Mexican liability insurance is a separate requirement from any driving document.
  • Federal-road speed guidance is published by SICT.

Mexico driving document questions

Do I need an International Driving Permit in Mexico?

It depends on your licence origin. UK guidance indicates a UK photocard licence may be usable without one. Other visitors should confirm with the rental provider and the relevant authority.

Do I need special insurance to drive in Mexico?

Mexican liability insurance is a separate legal requirement from any driving document and is normally arranged through the rental provider.
