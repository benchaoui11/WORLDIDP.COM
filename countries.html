Check before you travel.

Translation, licensing and document rules depend on the destination, your issuing country and the organization receiving the document. This guide helps you ask the right questions; it does not replace official advice.

Destination directory

Start with your destination

Every destination below requires individual verification. WorldIDP does not claim that a private translation is accepted or legally required there.

United States

Rules can differ by state and receiving organization. Check the relevant state authority and service provider.

Canada

Provincial and territorial requirements can differ. Verify with the province and intended recipient.

United Kingdom

Confirm current requirements with the relevant UK authority and any rental or insurance provider.

France

Ask the relevant French authority whether an official, sworn or private translation is appropriate.

Germany

Requirements depend on purpose and document origin. Confirm with the competent German authority.

Italy

Verify current document and translation rules before travel or presenting a document.

Spain

Check requirements with the relevant authority and the organization that will receive your document.

United Arab Emirates

Confirm requirements with the competent emirate authority and any intended service provider.

Thailand

Rules and enforcement can change. Check with the relevant Thai authority before relying on a translation.

Japan

Private translations may not meet official requirements. Verify the required document type in advance.

Australia

State and territory requirements can differ. Check the applicable authority and intended recipient.

New Zealand

Confirm whether the receiving organization requires an approved or certified translator.

Before ordering

Four questions to ask

01

Who will receive it?

A police authority, rental desk, employer, court and private business may each have different rules.

02

What type is required?

Ask whether the recipient needs a private, certified, sworn, notarized or government-issued document.

03

Which language?

Confirm the exact target language and whether transliteration or a particular format is required.

Know the requirement?
