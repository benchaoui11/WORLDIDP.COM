Service Disclaimer

Important facts about the nature and limits of a WorldIDP translation.

Last updated: July 11, 2026
Key pointsPrivate serviceOriginal requiredNo acceptance promiseNo advice
WorldIDP provides translation only. We do not create, replace or validate original records.

1. Independent private service

WORLDIDP INTERNATIONAL LLC is an independent private company. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by or acting on behalf of any public authority or receiving organization.

2. Translation does not replace the original

A WorldIDP translation is a language aid based on readable information from source material. It does not prove that the source material is authentic or current and must not be presented as the original record.

3. Acceptance is decided by others

Recipient requirements vary by organization, location and intended use. We do not guarantee that any third party will accept a private translation. Before ordering, ask the intended recipient what type of translation it requires.

4. No professional advice

Website content and customer support are general information only. For formal requirements, contact the receiving organization or a qualified professional.

5. Email request only

The request form prepares an email with basic request details only. It does not collect document files.
