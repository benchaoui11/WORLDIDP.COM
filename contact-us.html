WorldIDP provides a private multi-language translation of your valid national driver's license, prepared in the spirit of the 1949 Geneva and 1968 Vienna Conventions on Road Traffic to help you communicate your driving credentials abroad. It is carried together with — and never replaces — your original national license. WorldIDP is an independent private company and is not a government agency.
