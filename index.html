Straight answers

Before you request a translation

What if I need information about an International Driving Permit?

Read our independent educational guide and verify the requirement with the relevant authority. WorldIDP provides translation services and does not issue official documents.

Does the translation replace my original document?

No. A WorldIDP translation must be used only as a language aid and kept with the original valid document. It does not add rights, vehicle categories or validity.

Will every authority or business accept it?

No acceptance is guaranteed. Requirements vary. Ask the intended recipient whether it needs a private, certified, sworn or government-issued translation before ordering.

How is pricing confirmed?

Pricing depends on the document, language pair, format and urgency. We confirm the price and scope before paid work starts.

How should I send my document?

Start with the request form. Our team will explain the next step. Please do not email identity documents unless specifically instructed through an agreed secure process.
