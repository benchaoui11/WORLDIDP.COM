International Driving Permit quick facts

Clear answers before you apply for an IDP online

An International Driving Permit is useful when a traveler needs a multilingual driving licence translation abroad. It must be carried with a valid national driving licence, and local requirements can vary by country.

01

What an IDP does

It translates key licence details into a format that can be easier for foreign authorities and travel staff to read during document checks.

What is an International Driving Permit?
02

What an IDP does not do

It does not replace your national licence, override local road laws, or guarantee permission to drive in every destination.
