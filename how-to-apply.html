Before you start

The three things you need to apply

The customer application stays simple. These are the standard items WorldIDP uses to prepare the document from your valid original driver's license.

Valid driver's license

Your document is created from the valid original license you already hold. The upload screen may ask for both sides so the license details are readable.

Clear personal photo or selfie

Use a front-facing photo with good light and a clean background. You do not need to upload a passport unless another travel provider asks for it separately.

Signature

Add your signature in the upload step. It is used as part of the prepared driving-document translation.

1

What happens during upload and review

The upload step checks whether each required item has been added before the customer continues. Review is there to catch unclear materials before the document is approved.

  • License readability - all important license details must be visible and not blurred.
  • Photo clarity - the applicant's face should be clear, front-facing and well lit.
  • Signature present - the signature is added before payment and review.
2

Digital delivery and printed options

Digital is the fastest option. Printed orders add a physical document for travelers who want paper in hand or expect a third party to prefer it.

  • Digital document - delivered by email in approximately 5 minutes after successful review and approval.
  • Printed document - proceeds to production and delivery after the order is approved.
  • Format choice - destination rules and third-party requirements can vary, so choose the option that fits your trip.

Avoid delays

Common upload mistakes

Most delays come from images that are hard to read. These checks keep the application moving.

Blurred license images

Retake the photo if any license text, number or expiry date is unreadable.

Cropped license corners

Keep the whole license inside the image so all details can be checked.

Dark or side-facing selfie

Use a clear front-facing photo with enough light and a simple background.

Missing signature

Make sure the signature step shows complete before you continue to payment.

Clear expectations

What the document does and does not do

WorldIDP should feel simple, but the role of the document should still be clear.

What it does

  • Translates key details from your valid original driver's license.
  • Helps travel providers and reviewers understand your driving credentials.
  • Gives you digital and printed options for international travel preparation.

What it does not do

  • It does not replace your original valid driver's license.
  • It does not independently grant driving rights.
  • It does not guarantee acceptance by every country, police officer or rental company.
3

What to carry while driving abroad

The application only needs your license, photo and signature, but the documents you carry on the road can depend on your destination and travel provider.

  • Always carry your original valid driver's license.
  • Carry your supporting translated document where appropriate.
  • Bring any additional identification, insurance or rental paperwork required by the destination or third party.

Application FAQ

Common application questions

Short answers for the questions customers usually ask before they begin.

What documents do I need to apply?
You need a valid driver's license, a clear personal photo or selfie, and your signature. The upload page may ask you to photograph both sides of the license so the details can be read clearly.
Do I need to upload a passport?
No. WorldIDP asks for a clear personal photo or selfie, not a passport upload. You should still carry any identification required by your destination or travel provider when you drive abroad.
How fast is digital delivery?
After successful review and approval, the digital document is delivered by email in approximately 5 minutes. Printed orders proceed to production and delivery after approval.
Does the WorldIDP document replace my original license?
No. WorldIDP prepares a private multilingual driving-document translation. It must be carried with your original valid driver's license and does not independently grant driving rights. Read the legal disclaimer for details.
