This Cookie Policy explains how WorldIDP uses cookies and similar technologies, and gives you clear options to control them.

1What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences, keep you signed in, measure performance, and deliver relevant content. Similar technologies include pixels, local storage, and software development kits (SDKs).

2Types of cookies we use

Strictly necessaryRequired for the website and checkout to function — for example, keeping your order details as you move between steps. These cannot be switched off.
Performance & analyticsHelp us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it (e.g., which pages are most visited).
FunctionalRemember your preferences, such as language or country, to personalize your experience.
AdvertisingUsed by us and partners (including Google) to measure campaigns and show relevant ads, including based on your prior visits.

3Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by trusted third parties that provide services on our behalf, such as analytics providers, payment processors, and advertising partners like Google. These parties may use cookies to measure and improve their services and to deliver targeted advertising.

4How to manage cookies

You have full control over cookies:

  • Use our cookie banner to accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • Adjust or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.
  • Opt out of personalized Google ads at Google Ad Settings.
  • Opt out of third-party advertising cookies at aboutads.info or Your Online Choices.

Note: Disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent parts of the website, including checkout, from working correctly.

5Your consent

When you first visit our website, we ask for your consent to use non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time. For visitors in the EU and UK, we obtain consent before placing non-essential cookies, in line with applicable law.

6Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices or applicable laws change. The "Last updated" date above shows the most recent revision.
